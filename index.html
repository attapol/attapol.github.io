I am a Lecturer at the Department of Linguistics at Faculty of Arts Chulalongkorn University in Bangkok, Thailand. My current research interests are Thai core NLP infrastructure, NLP for Thai classical literature, deep learning techniques in NLP, discourse phenomena, and NLP in data science.

You can call me Te or อรรถพล. Thamrongrattanarit is my Thai last name. I adopted Rutherford as my new last name. This is confusing, I know.
