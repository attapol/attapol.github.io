Natural Language Processing Lab @Chula

Department of Linguistics, Faculty of Arts, Chulalongkorn University

NLP Lab currently focuses on developing robust Thai NLP infrastructure applicable to both old literature, contemporary language, and online digital media data. We are looking to apply NLP techniques to Digital Humanities and Computational Law.
