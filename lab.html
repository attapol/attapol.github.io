Natural Language Processing Lab @Chula

Department of Linguistics, Faculty of Arts, Chulalongkorn University

NLP Lab currently focuses on developing robust Thai NLP infrastructure applicable to both old literature, contemporary language, and online digital media data. We are looking to apply NLP techniques to Digital Humanities, Computational Law, and text analytics.

If you are a student who is interested in working my lab, please consider Master's program in Linguistics and choose แผน ข as your program for its better flexibility. The program will train you from writing your first program all the way to implementing your first neural language models. I draft up a sample study plan here.

If you are interested in doing a PhD in my lab, make sure to have the necessary background in programming and machine learning or email me to discuss a potential project.

I am always an opening for a postdoctoral researcher locally or from overseas. I expect the candidates to have completed a dissertation on machine learning and/or natural language processing.
